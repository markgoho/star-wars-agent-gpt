Star Wars Planets

  • Tatooine

    A desert planet located in the Outer Rim, Tatooine is known for its twin suns and its role in the Skywalker saga.

  • Alderaan

    A peaceful planet destroyed by the Death Star in A New Hope, Alderaan was home to Princess Leia Organa.

  • Hoth

    An icy planet that served as the Rebel Alliance's base in The Empire Strikes Back, Hoth is home to the tauntauns and the wampas.

  • Dagobah

    A swampy planet where Luke Skywalker trained with Jedi Master Yoda in The Empire Strikes Back and Return of the Jedi.

  • Endor

    A forest moon where the Rebel Alliance defeated the Empire in Return of the Jedi, Endor is home to the Ewoks.
